Chin Ups

Difficulty : Intermediate

  1. Grab the bar shoulderidth apart with a supinated grip (palms facing you)
  2. With your body hanging and arms fully extended, pull yourself up until your chin is past the bar.
  3. Slowly return to starting position. Repeat.

Dumbbells Curl

Difficulty : Beginner

  1. Stand up straight with a dumbbell in each hand at arm's length.
  2. Raise one dumbbell and twist your forearm until it is vertical and your palm faces the shoulder/li>
  3. Lower to original position and repeat with opposite arm

Dumbbells Hammer Curls

Difficulty : Beginner

  1. Hold the dumbbells with a neutral grip (thumbs facing the ceiling).

  3. Return to starting position and repeat.

Barbell Curl

Difficulty : Beginner

  2. Continue the movement until your Bicepscontracted and the bar is at shoulderl.

Reverse Barbell Curl

Difficulty : Advanced

  1. Take a double overhand grip that's about shoulder. Flex your elbows while keeping your elbows tucked in. Try not to let them flare out.
  2. Curl until your forearm presses into your bicep. Then fully extend your elbows at the bottom of each rep.
